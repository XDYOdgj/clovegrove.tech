Stainless Steel Suction Plates for Toddlers

These divided toddler plates are crafted from durable 304 stainless steel with a removable silicone suction base. Designed to stay firmly in place, the 4 non-slip cups prevent spills and messes, making mealtime easier and more enjoyable for both kids and parents. The non-toxic, BPA-free surface is safe for little hands and helps keep food fresh, encouraging healthy eating habits from an early age.

Perfect for use with highchair trays or tables, these plates offer excellent portion control and support a balanced diet. The three-section design lets babies mix and match foods, reducing picky eating while also cutting down on food waste. Ideal for snacks, fruits, veggies, pasta, and more, they're a long-lasting, eco-friendly alternative to plastic and help keep your child's hands clean during every meal.
