Avanchy Baby Plates for Mess-Free Meals and Safe Eating

Designed with a powerful suction base made from 100% silicone, these baby plates ensure a secure fit on most high chairs and tables. The non-toxic material is also unbreakable, making them safe for your little one's meals.

Each plate features a divided layout that helps serve different foods in separate sections. This promotes healthy eating habits and makes it easier for picky eaters to manage their meals. The plates are also dishwasher safe, offering effortless cleaning and saving you time and effort during feeding times.

Perfect for parents who want to simplify mealtime, these plates encourage independence and reduce spills. The suction technology keeps the plate steady as your child self-feeds, making cleanup a breeze. As your child grows, the removable silicone ring can be taken off, transforming the plate into a regular one for continued use.

Key Features:

  • Powerful suction base for secure attachment
  • Divided design to keep foods separate
  • BPA-free and non-toxic material
  • Dishwasher safe for easy cleaning
  • Removable silicone ring for future use

Whether you're looking for baby plates with suction, divided baby plates, or BPA-free toddler plates, these are the ideal tools for creating a balanced meal experience. They combine safety, durability, and convenience in one essential feeding accessory.
